About RK Knit Fab

RK Knit Fab is a fabric manufacturing company built on the principles of consistency, transparency, and long-term partnerships. We work closely with garment manufacturers and buyers who value quality, reliability, and clear communication.

Our journey began with a simple but strong focus — to manufacture knitted fabrics that perform consistently in real garment production. From yarn selection to knitting, finishing, and inspection, every step is managed with attention to detail.

Over the years, RK Knit Fab has grown by maintaining disciplined processes and by listening closely to our customers’ requirements. We understand that fabric is the foundation of any garment, and reliability at this stage is critical.

Today, we supply a range of knitted fabrics including cotton, poly-cotton, rib, and blended fabrics, supporting bulk orders with consistent quality and dependable timelines.
